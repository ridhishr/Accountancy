# 📘 **1. Branches of Accounting**

### **1. Financial Accounting**

* Records all financial transactions and prepares final accounts.
  👉 *Example:* Making Profit & Loss Account and Balance Sheet.

---

### **2. Cost Accounting**

* Calculates cost of production.
  👉 *Example:* Finding cost to make one product (₹50 per unit).

---

### **3. Management Accounting**

* Helps managers take decisions.
  👉 *Example:* Deciding whether to increase production or not.

---

### **4. Tax Accounting**

* Deals with calculation of tax.
  👉 *Example:* Calculating income tax of a business.

---

### **5. Social Responsibility Accounting**

* Shows impact of business on society.
  👉 *Example:* Company spending money on environment protection.

---

# 📘 **2. Nature of Accounting**

* It is a **process** → recording to final report
* It is an **art** → requires skill
* It is **based on rules**
* It deals with **financial information only**

👉 *Example:* Recording sales → preparing accounts → finding profit

---

# 📘 **3. Users of Accounting Information**

## **A. Internal Users (inside business)**

### 1. Owners

* Want to know profit and financial position
  👉 Example: Owner checks profit

### 2. Managers

* Use data for decision making
  👉 Example: Decide to expand business

---

## **B. External Users (outside business)**

### 1. Investors

* Want to know return on investment
  👉 Example: Check profit before investing

### 2. Creditors (suppliers)

* Want to know payment ability
  👉 Example: Will business repay money?

### 3. Government

* Wants tax information
  👉 Example: Checking tax records

### 4. Banks

* Want to know financial position
  👉 Example: Before giving loan

---

# 📘 **4. Advantages of Accounting**

1. **Keeps systematic records**
   👉 Example: All transactions recorded properly

2. **Helps in calculating profit/loss**
   👉 Example: Know business performance

3. **Shows financial position**
   👉 Example: Assets and liabilities shown

4. **Helps in decision making**
   👉 Example: Expand or not

5. **Useful for legal purposes**
   👉 Example: Tax filing

---

# 📘 **5. Limitations of Accounting**

1. **Records only financial data**
   👉 Example: Employee skills not recorded

2. **Based on estimates**
   👉 Example: Depreciation is estimated

3. **Ignores price changes**
   👉 Example: Inflation not considered properly

4. **Can be manipulated**
   👉 Example: Wrong profit shown

---

# 📘 **6. Qualitative Characteristics of Accounting Information**

### **1. Reliability**

* Information should be correct and trustworthy
  👉 Example: No false entries

---

### **2. Relevance**

* Information should be useful for decisions
  👉 Example: Profit data helps decision

---

### **3. Understandability**

* Easy to understand
  👉 Example: Simple reports

---

### **4. Comparability**

* Can compare with past or other businesses
  👉 Example: Compare this year profit with last year

---

### **5. Faithful Presentation**

* Shows true and fair view
  👉 Example: Real financial position shown

---
